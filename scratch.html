Books That Fueled My Growth

  1. "7 Habits of Highly Effective People" by Stephen Covey

    I learned seven habits that can help me become a better person.

  2. "The Compound Effect" by Darren Hardy

    The power of momentum is effort that compounds for or against you.

  3. "Man's Search For Meaning" by Victor Frankl

    I came to understand true perseverance in the face of horrific trials.

  4. "Atomic Habits" by James Clear

    Habits are the compound interest of self-improvement.

  5. "Make Your Bed" by Admiral William H. McRaven

    Beginning the day with a small victory, such as making your bed, sets you up for success throughout the day.
